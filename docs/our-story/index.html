Our Story

Welcome to Tigris Meditteranean Grille, where every dish is a voyage through the rich tapestry of time, history, and culture.

Our story begins in 2000, in the heart of Baghdad, Iraq—a city where the Tigris River has flowed for millennia, nurturing civilizations and shaping cultures. It was here, along the banks of this ancient river, that we first opened our doors, inspired by the rich culinary traditions of Mesopotamia and a deep love for the flavors of our homeland.

Our vision was clear: to bring the authentic tastes of the Middle East to life, crafting each dish in an ambiance that exudes warmth, hospitality, and the depth of our culture.

In 2003, as Baghdad entered a new chapter in its storied history, so did we. Amidst the challenges of a new era, we were honored to serve those who came from afar, including the US army and embassy. It was a time marked by both turmoil and hope, and through it all, our kitchen became a sanctuary—a place where the universal language of food transcended borders and brought people together.

But our journey was far from over. In 2005, Tigris Meditteranean Grille expanded to Amman, Jordan. Nestled in the vibrant streets of Amman, we quickly became a beacon for those seeking the true tastes of Iraq and the broader Middle East, combined with a touch of modern elegance. Our commitment to innovation and authenticity resonated with locals and visitors alike.

In 2012, we took another bold step, crossing oceans to open in San Diego, California. Here, we blended the rich flavors of our heritage with the diverse culinary landscape of the United States. While the Tigris River may be thousands of miles away, its spirit flows through every dish we serve, connecting our guests to the deep history and culture of Iraq.

At Tigris Meditteranean Grille, every meal is more than just food—it's a story. A connection to a culture as rich and deep as the river that inspires us. We invite you to join us and experience the flavors, stories, and traditions that have shaped our journey.
